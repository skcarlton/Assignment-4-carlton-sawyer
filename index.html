Golf is a wonderful sport that can be played at any age. It is an individual and international sport that requires players to get their ball into the hole by hitting it continuously down the course with a stick, also known as a club. This action is called taking a stroke. The person with the least strokes at the end of 18 holes, wins the game.

If you believe that golf is boring, that probably means you have not experienced it. If you have, and still think it is boring, that might be because you are not good at it yet. Lots of folks get discouraged by how challenging the game is, but those who overcome that frustration are better for it.
